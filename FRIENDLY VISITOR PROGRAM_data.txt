To promote and maintain an optimal level of elder independence through coordination of resources, advocacy, and caregiver support.
SSES is a private non-profit agency established in 1977. Based in Braintree,MA, we serve Braintree, and the surrounding towns of: Cohasset, Hingham, Holbrook, Hull, Milton, Norwelll, Quincy, Randolph, Scituate, Weymouth. We are committed to providing a wide range of programs and services to elders over the age of 60, and their caregivers. We work closely with each consumer to deliver services that reflect individual needs, personal choice, and self-determination.
